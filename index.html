Legal & procedural FAQs

What legal basis allows recovery of crypto from illegitimate platforms?
English courts have recognised cryptocurrency as property (AA v Persons Unknown [2019], Ion Science v Persons Unknown [2020]). We use proprietary claims, unjust enrichment, and constructive trust arguments.
What are the costs and funding options?
Initial assessment: £750+VAT. Thereafter, clients choose: (a) hourly rates £450-£650+VAT, or (b) contingency fee agreement (15-20% of recovered sum, only for meritorious fraud cases). Disbursements (court fees, expert reports) are separate.
How do you enforce against offshore exchanges?
We obtain worldwide freezing orders from the English High Court, which can be recognised and enforced in many offshore jurisdictions (BVI, Cayman, Singapore) under common law principles or reciprocal arrangements.
What information do I need to start a case?
Transaction hash(es), wallet addresses, platform name/URL, screenshots of communications, and approximate amount lost. The more on-chain data, the faster we can trace.
